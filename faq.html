FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about Lahore Zoo

1. What are the opening hours of Lahore Zoo?

Lahore Zoo is open from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM daily, including weekends and public holidays.

2. How much is the entry fee?

The entry fee for Lahore Zoo varies for different age groups and categories. Please refer to the latest fee schedule posted at the entrance or check the official website for updated information.

3. Are there any discounts for students or senior citizens?

Yes, Lahore Zoo offers discounted entry fees for students and senior citizens upon presentation of valid identification. Please inquire at the ticket counter for details.

4. Can visitors bring outside food and drinks?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside Lahore Zoo premises. However, there are food stalls and refreshment areas available inside the zoo where visitors can purchase snacks and beverages.

5. Are there guided tours available?

Yes, Lahore Zoo offers guided tours for groups upon prior arrangement. Please contact the zoo administration for booking and scheduling.

6. Is photography allowed inside the zoo?

Photography for personal use is permitted inside Lahore Zoo. However, visitors are advised to respect the animals and avoid using flash photography, as it may disturb them.

7. Are there any restrictions on touching or feeding the animals?

For the safety of both visitors and animals, touching or feeding the animals is strictly prohibited inside Lahore Zoo.

8. Is there parking available?

Yes, Lahore Zoo has parking facilities for visitors' convenience. However, parking space may be limited during peak hours, so it's advisable to arrive early.

9. Are there wheelchair-accessible facilities?

Yes, Lahore Zoo is wheelchair-accessible, with ramps and pathways designed to accommodate visitors with mobility challenges.

10. Are there any rules or guidelines visitors should be aware of?

Yes, visitors are expected to follow the zoo's rules and guidelines, which include refraining from littering, maintaining a respectful distance from the animals, and following instructions from zoo staff for their own safety and the well-being of the animals. For any further inquiries or assistance, please feel free to contact Lahore Zoo administration.
